Meal Replacement Smoothie

Again, I am boring when it comes to my eating habits. Most days for my lunch, I make a boring smoothie with a meal replacement powder. I get all of the nutrients I need in what I consider an efficient and inexpensive manner. I also do not have the burden of trying to come up with something enjoyable and flavorful each day, which I consider exhausting.

I personally use Huel black edition vanilla as my meal replacement. I work out a lot and frequently rock climb, so I like the extra protein to help fuel my muscles. Vanilla is also the most flavorless for my deprived taste buds, but you can get as adventuress as you would like!

  1. Grab the blender
  2. Start by adding the meal replacement powder
  3. At this point, use whatever berries you would like! I opt for a small portion of blueberries most days
  4. Next, we add the liquids! I like to include a splash of cold brew, but the main ingredient here is (for me) a plant-based milk with added protein
  5. Blend it up and enjoy! Or don't, I just try to chug it down and get on with my day
